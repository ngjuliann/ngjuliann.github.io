About Julian Ng

Julian Ng has also been fascinated by the live production scene. He has gotten involved with drama productions, musical, and concerts since he was of the age of thirteen. Julian started on a small analogue system, and has moved up to a fully-featured digital mixer.

It was from this musical where lightning started to grow on Julian. Starting out on a basic system, he started designing lighting for drama productions. Julian would spend numerous hours just focusing lights aiming for perfection.
